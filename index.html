Nice to meet you!

My name is Manisha Panta. I am a Ph.D. student with a research concentration in Machine Learning, Deep Learning for Computer Vision, and Bioinformatics. I perform massive experimental research in computational biology and environmental informatics problem domains.


My research mainly focuses on understanding faults in the levee system by identifying and localizing them using images, sensor data, and videos collected from Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAVs). I occasionally collaborate with other domain researchers in tourism and web security to leverage my AI knowledge to solve problems in those sectors.


Moreover, I feel rewarded to work in the field where technology directly connects with the societal aspects; the idea that I am contributing to society's greater good while doing something I love is very satisfying.


I am a proud member of Nepali Women in Computing (NWiC), one of AnitaB Systers Affinity Groups.


I am super excited to be (Women in Data Science) WiDS Ambassador Nepal 2021.
